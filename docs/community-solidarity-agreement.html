Community Solidarity Agreement

Established: June 2026 · Publicly posted and revisable

This is a public commitment about how cyborgdreamz distributes its proceeds. It is not a legal contract with any individual. It is a community accountability document — posted publicly so that people who engage with this work can hold it to its stated values.

Why this exists

This work — the quiz, the workbooks, the skill deck, the community spaces — exists at the intersection of clinical practice, erotic liberation, and digital ethics. It is built on a politics that names mutual aid and economic solidarity as inseparable from care work.

Posting revenue commitments publicly is an accountability practice, not a marketing move. It reflects a belief that how money moves is part of the ethics of the work — not separate from it.

This agreement governs how net proceeds from cyborgdreamz products and community memberships are distributed during the current phase of Soleil Merroir's housing and economic stabilization.

Net proceeds means gross revenue minus: Stripe processing fees, Brevo subscription costs, Make.com subscription costs, Netlify/hosting costs, and any direct production costs (design tools, printing if applicable). Distribution commitment

Phase Condition Exchange Retained
Phase 1 — Current Until stable long-term housing is secured for Soleil and their child 50% of net proceeds 50%

Phase 3 — Ongoing Sustained, indefinitely 10% of net proceeds 90%

The 10% sustained commitment is a floor, not a ceiling. Additional contributions may be made at any time at Soleil's discretion or in response to community need.

What the Exchange receives

Proceeds directed to the Exchange may take the form of: direct financial transfer, equivalent goods or services at agreed value within a time bank or barter network, contribution to a community fund, or in-kind contribution (sessions, training, facilitation) at the Exchange's request.

The Exchange is notified of each disbursement. Disbursement schedule: quarterly, or monthly once revenue is consistent. Records of disbursements are available to community members on request.

What this does not cover

This agreement applies to net proceeds from digital products (quiz, workbooks, skill deck), community memberships, and mixer events. It does not apply to: clinical session fees (governed by licensing and insurance obligations), speaking fees, CE course revenue from platforms like PESI (governed by platform agreements), or grant funding.

Clinical session fees are Soleil's primary income and are not subject to this agreement. This agreement applies specifically to the cyborgdreamz product and community revenue stream.

Accountability

This document is posted publicly at soleilmerroir.com/solidarity and updated when conditions change. Community members may request a summary of disbursements at any time by emailing hello@soleilmerroir.com.

If this commitment is not being honored, community members are encouraged to name it directly — in the Discord, in email, or publicly. This document exists to be held to.

Amendment

This agreement may be amended by Soleil Merroir at any time. Amendments that reduce the Exchange percentage below Phase 3 minimum (10%) require a 30-day public notice period. Amendments that increase the Exchange percentage may be made immediately. All amendments are dated and noted in a revision history below.
